PREVENTIVE MEASURES

Reducing carbon footprints is essential to mitigate climate change and promote environmental sustainability. Here are some preventive measures individuals, businesses, and governments can adopt to minimize their carbon footprints

Energy Efficiency:

-Use energy-efficient appliances and LED lights.
- Insulate your home for heating and cooling savings.
- Unplug electronics and opt for renewables like solar or wind power.

Transportation:

-Use public transport, carpool, or bike.
-Choose fuel-efficient or electric vehicles.
- Walk for short trips to reduce emissions.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle:

Reduce waste by buying products with minimal packaging.
- Reuse items like bags, containers, and water bottles.
- Recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metal to reduce landfill waste.

Water Conservation:

- Fix leaks and use water-saving appliances.
- Take shorter showers and turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.
- Collect rainwater for plants and garden use.
