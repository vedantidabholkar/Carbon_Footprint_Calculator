RELATED ORGANISATION

Organizations are vital for reducing carbon footprints as they can make a significant environmental impact, lead in innovation, save costs, comply with regulations, build trust with consumers, enhance climate resilience, and fulfill global responsibilities.

Reliance Industries

Mukesh Ambani's Reliance Industries aims to be carbon-free by 2035, stressing the need for sustainability and plans to contribute 100 GW to India's 450 GW renewable energy target by 2030.

Tata Consultancy Services

The company aims for net-zero emissions by 2030, with a 70% reduction in greenhouse gas emissions (Scope 1 and 2) by 2025. They plan to leverage digital innovation for sustainability and collaborate with various stakeholders for a sustainable future.

Mahindra & Mahindra

Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M), a prominent Indian vehicle manufacturer, plans to be carbon-neutral by 2040. They will prioritize energy efficiency, renewable power, and address residual emissions through carbon sinks..

ITC

ITC, a multi-business conglomerate, aims to achieve 100% renewable energy use by 2030 through digitalization and cross-sector collaborations. ITC Windsor, an ITC Hotels property in Bengaluru, became the first hotel globally to attain LEED Zero Carbon Certification in March.
